How this project works..

Have you ever seen this sign before?

It's a common sign around garbage cans, indicating the need for sorting waste. However, it's often too much of a hassle to sort waste while disposing of it. That's why we aim to simplify the process through our project. By simply scanning a product's barcode, users will receive information about the item and how to properly dispose of it. They'll be informed whether specific parts of the product should be composted, recycled, or sent to a landfill.
